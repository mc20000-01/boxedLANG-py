# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The boxedLANG project is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all those who participate in our community, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

## Expected Behavior

All contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and inclusive toward all participants
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy toward other community members
- Provide and accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing opinions and experiences
- Work collaboratively with others
- Help maintain a safe and welcoming environment

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, ability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, or any other protected characteristic
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Violent threats or language
- Insults, slurs, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Plagiarism or academic dishonesty
- Spam or promotional content unrelated to the project
- Disrupting discussions or meetings

## Consequences

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face consequences, including:

- A warning for minor violations
- Temporary or permanent suspension from project participation
- Removal from the community
- Reporting to appropriate authorities for illegal behavior

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience violations of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document the behavior** - Note what happened, when, and who was involved if safe to do so
2. **Contact us** - Report to the project maintainers via:
   - Email to the project maintainers
   - Private message to maintainers
   - GitHub issue (if the issue is safe to share publicly)
3. **Provide details** - Include as much relevant information as possible while protecting your safety

All reports will be treated confidentially and investigated promptly. The project maintainers are committed to protecting the identity of reporters and taking appropriate action.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the boxedLANG project, including:

- Project repositories on GitHub
- Issues and pull requests
- Project discussions
- Community forums or chat
- In-person events associated with the project

This Code of Conduct also applies to representatives of the project in public spaces when they are acting in an official capacity.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They have the right and responsibility to:

- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions
- Ban individuals who violate this Code of Conduct
- Take other appropriate action in response to violations
